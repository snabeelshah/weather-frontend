Meet Our Team - NST Edge

NST Edge is a team of students from the NST Society at NED University, participating in the NASA Space Apps Challenge. Our project, “Will It Rain on My Parade?”, focuses on analyzing weather to provide useful insights. With diverse skills in development, research, and communication, we work together to build innovative and practical solutions under NST's guidance.
